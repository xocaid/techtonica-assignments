Creamy Lemon Garlic Pasta

I was trying to figure out what to make with a lemon asides from lemonade and I
came across this recipe on social media and then modified to add some extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of dry pasta(your choice)
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 chopped onion
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 lemon zest
  • 1 lemon juice squeezed
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley or cilantro
  • 1 chopped serrano
  • black pepper to taste
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chicken bouillon

Cooking Instructions

  1. Place a pot of water, add salt, and cook pasta on high heat until pasta is al dente.
  2. Separately in a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic, onion, and serrano and cook until fragant and stir constantly.
  3. Add in flour and stir until golden, make sure not to burn it.
  4. Lower heat to low and stir in heavy cream. Season sauce with salt, black pepper and chicken bouillon, continue stirring until sauce thickens.
  5. Stir in lemon zest, squeeze lemon juice, parmesan cheese and parsley/cilantro into sauce.
  6. Once pasta is ready, drain water - reserve a cup of pasta water for sauce- and add to sauce.
  7. Mix pasta and sauce well, if sauce is too thick, add reserved pasta water to thin out.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
